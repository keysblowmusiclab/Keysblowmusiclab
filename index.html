About

Key Blow Music Lab is led by Juan Gutiérrez, a professional clarinetist and dedicated educator based in Gold Coast. With years of experience in performance and teaching, including time with the Bogotá Philharmonic Orchestra, Juan offers expert, personalised instruction for students of all ages and levels.

What We Offer

  • Woodwind Lessons: Clarinet, Flute, Saxophone, Oboe
  • Piano Lessons: For beginners to advanced players
  • Flexible Formats: In-person and online options
  • Child-Safe: Blue Card holder, trusted by families
  • Tailored Programs: For school auditions, AMEB exams, and personal growth
