About Juan

Juan Gutiérrez is a professional clarinetist and dedicated music educator based in Gold Coast. With experience as a performer in the Bogotá Philharmonic Orchestra and as a conductor and teacher in various institutions, he brings expertise, passion and structure to every lesson.

What We Offer

  • Clarinet: technique, phrasing, expression
  • Flute: tone, breathing, posture
  • Saxophone: sound, articulation, confidence
  • Oboe: basics, fingerings, control
  • Piano: classical & contemporary, expressiveness
  • Formats: In-person & online, child-safe with Blue Card
